GOVERNMENT STATUTORY INVESTIGATION REPORT — EXHIBIT #1078
INVESTIGATING AGENCY: OISD Refinery Safety Directorate
HAZARD CATEGORY: Simultaneous Hot Work Spark Explosion
REGULATORY STATUTE VIOLATED: OISD-STD-018 Clause 8.1

INCIDENT SUMMARY:
On industrial shift operations, supervisory safety interlocks recorded a catastrophic containment breach in Plant Sector Zone-1. Continuous multi-gas IoT telemetry registered peak atmospheric contaminant concentrations of 91 PPM, drastically exceeding permissible exposure ceilings.

CAUSAL MECHANISM & ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
Forensic engineering evaluation determined that the primary failure mode was: Welding spark ignited hydrocarbon vapors from unisolated vent line (<15m proximity). Simultaneous Operations (SIMOPS) density compounding inadequate dedicated safety officer oversight prevented timely emergency evacuation.

MANDATORY CORRECTIVE & PREVENTIVE ACTIONS (CAPA):
1. Install hard-wired digital interlocks between portable gas monitors and PTW authorization servers.
2. Enforce rigid 15-meter radial exclusion buffer zones during active hot work SIMOPS.
3. Mandate redundant backup battery power feeds for all mechanical confined space exhaust blowers.
